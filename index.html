about us

1.

Who we are

Arj plywood is a type of engineered wood made from thin layers of wood veneer glued together, known for its strength and durability.

2.

What do we do

ARJ Plywood is a high-quality, durable plywood brand used for furniture, construction, and interiors. It is moisture-resistant, termite-proof, and long-lasting.

3.

how do we help

At ARJ Plywood, we provide premium-quality plywood solutions for furniture, interiors, and construction. Our products are durable, termite-resistant, and moisture-proof, ensuring long-lasting performance for your projects.

4.

success story

ARJ Plywood started with a vision to provide durable, high-quality plywood. Through innovation, eco-friendly materials, and advanced technology, we became a trusted name in the industry. Our termite-resistant, waterproof, and long-lasting plywood is used in homes and commercial projects. Today, ARJ Plywood stands for strength, reliability, and excellence.
